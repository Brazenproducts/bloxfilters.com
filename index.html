Why Facilities Are Switching to BLOX™

Reusable Housings

With most coatings, only the patented BowTie® filter inserts need to be swapped. The BLOX™ housings stay in place, saving hours of cleanup per trolley per shift and reducing waste significantly.

No Fiber Contamination

BLOX™ uses 100% recycled non-fibrous corrugate for the filter housing. No fiber particles are introduced to the air, protecting finish quality.

Flat-Pack Assembly

Housings arrive folded flat. A simple press forms them into a cube that slides into the trolley. Patented BowTie® filters slide into the reusable housings. Saves space and hours of labor.

Engineered to Hold

Tested to withstand well over the maximum airflow without failing, keeping internal components cleaner for easier change-outs.

Works with Grid Guard®

Used with Walk Industrial's reusable patented Grid Guard® system, change-outs of the BLOX™ system are quicker and easier than any other filter made for the Dürr® EcoDry X trolley.

Made in the USA

Designed and manufactured by Walk Industrial with the same engineering quality behind the patented BowTie® filter platform.

How It Works

The BLOX™ system separates the filter housing from the filter media. The corrugate housing forms the structural container that slides into the Dürr® trolley. Inside, patented BowTie® filter inserts handle the actual overspray capture.

When filters load up, only the BowTie® inserts need to be replaced. The BLOX™ housing stays in place with most coating types, turning what used to be a full teardown into a simple swap.
